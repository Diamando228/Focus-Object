WHAT DEFINES THIS PROTOTYPE
Prototype overview
  • Positioning An object for a mindful entry into a work session. Used on the desk during individual, focused work.
  • Design An atypical timer form - restrained and neutral. The main accent is not the screen, but the camera as the central element.
  • Control Session start happens via a gesture read by the camera. Time is set with a central rotary control.
  • Behavior Focus begins with a conscious gesture. The camera creates a sense of presence - no distracting numbers, but a reminder that the process has started.
